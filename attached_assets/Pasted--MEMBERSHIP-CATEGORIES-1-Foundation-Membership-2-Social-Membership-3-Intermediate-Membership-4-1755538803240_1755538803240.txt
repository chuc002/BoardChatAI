Nonresident Membership **FEE STRUCTURES** 1. **Foundation Membership:** - Initiation Fee: 100% of the current initiation fee - Transfer Fee: Equal to the initiation fee for Foundation (Legacy) Membership - Monthly Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Capital Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Eligibility Criteria: By Board approval - Privileges: Full access to all Club facilities - Restrictions: None specified - Approval Process: Board approval required 2. **Social Membership:** - Initiation Fee: 75% of the current initiation fee - Transfer Fee: Not specified - Monthly Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Capital Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Eligibility Criteria: By Board approval - Privileges: Limited access to Club facilities - Restrictions: None specified - Approval Process: Board approval required 3. **Intermediate Membership:** - Initiation Fee: 50% of the current initiation fee - Transfer Fee: Not specified - Monthly Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Capital Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Age Requirement: Under 24 years old - Eligibility Criteria: By Board approval - Privileges: Limited access to Club facilities - Restrictions: None specified - Approval Process: Board approval required 4. **Legacy Membership:** - Initiation Fee: Not specified - Transfer Fee: Not specified - Monthly Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Capital Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Eligibility Criteria: By Board approval - Privileges: Full access to all Club facilities - Restrictions: None specified - Approval Process: Board approval required 5. **Corporate Membership:** - Initiation Fee: Not specified - Transfer Fee: Not specified - Monthly Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Capital Dues: Specified in the Bylaws - Eligibility Criteria: By Board approval - Privileges: Full access to all Club facilities - Restrictions: None specified - Approval Process: Board approval required 6. **Golfing Senior Membership:** - Initiation Fee: 25% of the current initiation fee - Transfer Fee: Not specified - Monthly Dues: Specified in the Bylaws -
